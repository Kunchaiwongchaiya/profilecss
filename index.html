I started working and earning money in the entertainment. As a result, I am more ambitious than other people and have a mature mindset unlike other people. After I became like everyone else, it made me "stop" to develop myself and indulge in the happiness that was there. I do not know how to keep and it is the period that is known as the height of the fall, it only hurts. Then came the words that made me begin to transform myself with the words, “Can't do that?” “You never will be the boss.” Those words made me think and take the initiative to change myself until now. I will continue to evolve infinitely and not to return to the same place. Finally, all my efforts have never been in vain but will pay back for what I have done. In addition to teaching me about experience, I also learned a lot. In 2018, I made a lot of changes in myself. I setting high dreams that even I couldn't see. I have been rewarded a lot for what I have done. In 2019, I was referred to as the “Nine Chair”. The word chair doesn't come just for competition but the word president is a must-have for people to accept. It's not easy for me from someone who has nothing to these day and I will be role model to someone like me. Today I started And have you started yet?
